Who it is for

Best for a business that updates its site regularly: new menu items, seasonal hours, events, photos, or staff changes. You want a real person who already knows your site and turns changes around quickly.

What is included

What you get with Tend

Everything in Lookout

All the hosting, domain, SSL, security, and backup care from the Lookout plan is included as the foundation.

Content updates within days

Send me your changes (text, photos, hours, events, a new team member) and I will usually have them live within a couple of business days. Regular, ongoing updates are part of the plan.

Contact form & email managed

I keep your contact or order form working, watch for delivery problems, and make sure messages actually reach the right inbox.

Simple monthly note from me

Once a month you get a short, plain note: what I changed, that backups and security are healthy, and anything worth your attention. No jargon and no dashboard to log into.

Good to know

Content updates cover the normal upkeep of your site, and a typical month is a handful of requests. Unusually large or bulk work, like loading dozens of items at once, is quoted first so there are never any surprises. Third-party costs such as premium services or stock photos are passed through at cost.

What is not included

How requests work

Just tell me what you need

No tickets and no portal to log into. Email or text me what you want changed. I confirm I have it and tell you when to expect it done. On Tend, content updates are typically live within two to three business days. I reply to messages within about one business day.

1. Send it over

A quick email or text with the change. A photo, a new price, a paragraph, whatever it is.

2. I confirm

I reply (usually within one business day) to say I have it and when it will be done.

3. It goes live

I make the change, check it on desktop and phone, and let you know it is up.

4. If it is bigger

If a request is really a project, I tell you up front and give a flat quote before doing anything.
