Questions

Good things to know

How long does a website take?
Most small business sites are ready in about a week once I have your content and photos. Larger sites take a little longer, and I will give you a clear timeline up front.
Do I own my site and domain?
Yes. The domain is registered in your name, and the site is yours. If you ever move on, you keep everything.
What if I just need a few changes later?
That is what the monthly plans are for. Send me what you need changed and I will take care of it, usually within a few days.
Do you work with churches and nonprofits?
Often. A good amount of my work is for churches, ministries, and small local organizations, and I am happy to work within a tight budget.
What does it cost to get started?
Builds start at $600 one-time, then a monthly care plan from $30. I will give you a flat quote before any work begins, so there are no surprises.
