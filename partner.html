Who it is for

Best for a business whose website does real work, and that wants steady, proactive attention rather than only fixing things when they are noticed.

What is included

What you get with Partner

Everything in Tend

All the upkeep, fast content updates, managed forms, and monthly notes from the Tend plan, included.

Scheduled content & new pages

Beyond edits, I add new content on a schedule we agree on: a monthly update or post, event listings, and new standard pages built in your existing design.

Priority turnaround

Your requests go to the front of the line, typically handled within one business day.

Quarterly check-in & ideas

Every quarter we have a short call or note: what is working, what to freshen up, and a few specific ideas to help the site pull its weight.

How requests work

Just tell me what you need

No tickets and no portal to log into. Email or text me what you want changed. I confirm I have it and tell you when to expect it done. On Partner, your requests get priority and are typically handled within one business day. Scheduled content runs on the cadence we agree on.

1. Send it over

A quick email or text with the change. A photo, a new price, a paragraph, whatever it is.

2. I confirm

I reply (usually within one business day) to say I have it and when it will be done.

3. It goes live

I make the change, check it on desktop and phone, and let you know it is up.

4. If it is bigger

If a request is really a project, I tell you up front and give a flat quote before doing anything.
